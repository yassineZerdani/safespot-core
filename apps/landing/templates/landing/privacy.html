SafeSpot ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our safety services:

  • Location data: Used to show safety scores, display your position on the map, and provide location-based alerts. Location is only used when you grant permission and while the app is in use.
  • Reported content: When you submit an incident report, we store the type, description, and approximate location you provide.
  • Device information: We may collect device type and operating system for compatibility and support.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide safety scores and incident alerts for your area
  • Display community-reported incidents on the map
  • Show emergency numbers for your location
  • Improve our services and fix issues

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share aggregated, anonymized data for research or service improvement. Incident reports are shared with other users as part of the community safety feature.

4. Data Retention

We retain incident reports and location data as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. Transmission is encrypted and we follow secure development practices.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@safespot.app.

7. Children

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy: support@safespot.app
